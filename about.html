Our Heritage & Mission

Mntambo Safety Services and Construction is a proud 100% Black Empowerment Company established in 2018. We are dedicated to uplifting our community through exceptional service, expert craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to safety and quality.


With years of experience in ISO systems, SHEQ management, specialized training, and custom furniture manufacturing, we partner with businesses to build safer, more efficient operations while delivering high-end solutions tailored to your needs.
